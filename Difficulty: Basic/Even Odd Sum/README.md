<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/even-odd-sum5450/1?page=6&difficulty=Basic&status=unsolved&sortBy=submissions">Even Odd Sum</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Basic</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO" style="--text-color: var(--problem-text-color);"><p><span style="font-size: 18px;">Given an array arr[] of integers, find the sum of values of even and odd positions where a position is index + 1.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Examples:</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input:</strong> arr[] = [1, 2, 3, 4, 5]
<strong>Output: </strong>[6, 9]
<strong>Explanation: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">Sum of elements at even places i.e. at 2nd and 4th places is (2 + 4 = 6). </span><span style="font-size: 18px;">Sum of elements at odd places i.e at 1st,3rd and 5th places is (1 + 3 + 5 = 9). </span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong>arr[] = [1, 1, 1, 1, 1] 
<strong>Output: </strong>[2, 3]
<strong>Explanation: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">Sum of elements at even places is (1+1=2). S</span><span style="font-size: 18px;">um of elements at odd places is (1+1+1=3).</span></pre>
